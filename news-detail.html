Alkor Bio Group of Companies received the Federal Service for Surveillance in Healthcare of Russia for a kit of reagents for the qualitative enzyme immunoassay of immunoglobulins M

Registration Certificate (H1)

In the middle of September, the Alkor Bio Group of Companies (St. Petersburg), a Russian developer and manufacturer of test-systems and equipment for laboratory diagnostics by ELISA, IHLA and PCR methods, received a registration certificate from the Federal Service for Surveillance in Healthcare (Roszdravnadzor) for new kit "SARS-CoV-2 ELISA-IgM" for the qualitative enzyme immunoassay for the determination of immunoglobulins M to the SARS-CoV-2 in human serum and plasma.
